Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a dish that consists of chicken, fettucini noodles but it can be any choice of pasta, and a creamy cheese sauce

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Start boiling a pot of water big enough to hold all of the pasta
  2. While the water is boiling, start to cook the chicken
  3. Once the water is boiling, add the pasta and cook it to the box's instructions for time
  4. As the chicken begins to get close to being cooked, test it with a thermometer and make sure that it reaches an internal temp of 160 degrees F
  5. In the pan that you cooked the chicken in, melt the butter over low heat
  6. Once the butter is starting to foam, begin to add in the cream. Whisk to combine
  7. Add in the parmesan, and whisk until it begins to thicken. Once the sauce is thickened, combine the chicken, pasta and sauce.
