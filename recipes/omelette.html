Omelette

Description

An omelette is an egg based dish that can range anywhere from just plain and simple eggs all the way to being filled with whatever ingredients that you want

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • butter
  • any other add-ins that you would want

Steps:

  1. Mix 2-3 eggs in a seperate container with a splash of milk
  2. Heat butter in a pan until foaming
  3. Add in the eggs on a low heat
  4. Gently move the eggs in the pan in order to make layers to build large curd
  5. once the eggs are almost done, add in whatever mix-ins that you would like and gently fold the eggs in half to sandwich the mix-ins
  6. When the eggs are done, gently transfer them to a plate
