Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a dish that consists of red sauce, pasta, meat and cheese layered together to make the final dish

Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzerella Cheese

Steps:

  1. Boil the pasta in the water
  2. Cook the ground beef in a pan, season it with italian seasonings
  3. in a sheet pan, layer sauce, pasta, meat, cheese continuously until out of all ingredients
  4. bake until the dish is warmed through and all of the cheese has melted
